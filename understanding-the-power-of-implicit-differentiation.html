Example: Find $(\arcsin x)'$.

Solution:

Let $y=\arcsin x$, then $x=\sin y$, and differentiate both sides, we get:

$$\begin{aligned}\cos y dy&=dx\\\frac{dy}{dx}&=\frac1{\cos y}\end{aligned}$$

We can draw a right triangle to help us solve for $y$:

This yields:

$$\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac1{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$$
